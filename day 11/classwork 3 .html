leopard

Leopard is one of the five extant species in the genus panthera It has a pale yellowish to dark golden fur with dark spots grouped in rosettes. The Leopard was first described in 1758. Leopard is very dangerous animal. Felis or Leopardus was used as the generic name until the early 20th century. Leopards eat meat. In 1953, a male leopard and a female lion were crossbred in Hanshin Park in Nishinomiya, Japan.
